About Us

We are a family owned BBQ restaurant who have revamped their restaurant. With a larger and more open layout we can have four times as many guest as before. Due to our larger size we are now employing even more people than before.

Unlike all other BBQ restaurants, we get everything from our own farm. All our supplies come in fresh in the morning and are cooked to order. Since all of our foods are freshly made the wait time is longer but not by a substantial amount, only around 5 minutes.
