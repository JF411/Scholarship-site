FAQ

Are masks mandatory to enter?

Masks are not mandatory if vaccinated, if you do not have a mask we will provide you with one. You will not have to keep the mask on while eating.

Do we need to make a reservation?

No, you do not need to make a reservation. However you can expect a wait of 30 minutes up to an hour depending on staffing and any ongoing event.

How early in advance should we make our reservation?

24-48 hours is preferred but it can also be done on the day of.

Is there delivery available and is there a fee for it?

Yes, we do deliveries and the fee is based on how far you are from our location.

Are you wheelchair friendly?

Yes, we are wheelchair accessible.
